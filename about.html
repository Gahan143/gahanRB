About R.B Engineering

Engineering excellence on wheels since 2005

Our Story

Founded in 2017 by Bishnu Prasad Adhikari, R.B Engineering began as a small workshop in Pokhara with a vision to transform Nepal's automotive industry through quality craftsmanship and innovation.

What started as a modest operation with just three employees has grown into Pokhara's premier bus body manufacturing and vehicle restoration facility, employing over 20+ skilled professionals.

"Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has been the driving force behind our growth. Every vehicle we build or restore carries our reputation."

— Bishnu Prasad Adhikari, Founder
